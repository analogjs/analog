## Sponsoring

<GithubSponsorsLogo width="120" height="120" />

You can [sponsor Analog on GitHub Sponsors](https://github.com/sponsors/brandonroberts).

As a thank you for sponsorships of at least USD 50 per month you can get a logo added to [the Analog README](https://github.com/analogjs/analog#readme) on GitHub and npm.

Sponsorships of at least USD 100 per month earns the addition of a logo to [the Analog README](https://github.com/analogjs/analog#readme) on GitHub and npm as well as the homepage of the website. Additionally, you will receive thanks for your sponsorship in blog posts about Analog.

For monthly sponsorships of greater amounts, contact [the Analog core team](/docs/contributors#analog-core-team) to discuss sponsorship opportunities further.
